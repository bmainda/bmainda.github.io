Game drives & Air balloon

Every year, millions of wildebeest, zebras, and other animals migrate from the Serengeti National Park in Tanzania to the Maasai Mara in Kenya in search of pastures and water. The migration creates a great spectacle as the herbivores are hunted, stalked and run down by larger carnivores and in the end, only the most resilient survive. Despite the Massacre, millions still make it across the Mara River only to make the same journey back to Serengeti in three months.

Kenya hot air balloon safari is a luxurious way to experience the most scenic national parks and game reserves, especially the great migration, also considered one of the 8 wonders of the world. Even better, the ride costs about $500 per person, including a champagne breakfast in the bush.
