Kenya

Kenya is a country in East Africa with coastline on the Indian Ocean. It encompasses savannah, lakelands, the dramatic Great Rift Valley and mountain highlands. It's also home to wildlife like lions, elephants and rhinos


Things to do in Kenya

A game drive is an adventure that entails viewing wildlife in the comfort of a 4×4 open-sided safari vehicle that accommodates 4 to 6 people. Safari game drives are one of the most popular ways to see wildlife in the Game parks.At Basecamp game drives are usually conducted in the early mornings, late afternoon or at night, since these are the coolest times of the day when most animals are more active.A tour guide will introduce you to life in the savanna and share their infinite knowledge about African wildlife.

Ending your safari trip with a visit to the Kenyan coast is the ultimate safari holiday. Diani Beach is located about 30 km south of Mombasa. It is regarded as Kenya’s finest beach, extending to about 25km of clear ocean and white sandy beaches

Facts about Kenya

Although there are two official languages,Kiswahili and Englis, there are around 68 languages spoken throughout the country. Greet the locals in Kiswahili with "Jambo" to say hello, or "Habari" for how are you?

Lake Turkana is the world's largest desert lake

Mount Kenya is the second highest mountain in Africa.
